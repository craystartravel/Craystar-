Meet the Advisor

Eric Crayton

Owner of Craystar Travel Agency, based in Springfield, Missouri and serving travelers across all 50 states. Eric founded Craystar with a clear mission: take the overwhelm out of vacation planning by giving every traveler access to a real, certified advisor — at no cost.

Eric's specializations are Walt Disney World, Universal Orlando, and Royal Caribbean cruises, but Craystar plans every kind of trip — Disneyland, Disney Cruise Line, Aulani, Adventures by Disney, Universal Hollywood, every major cruise line, and the top all-inclusive resort brands across the Caribbean and Mexico.

In 2026, Eric earned the SNG Certified Accessible Travel Advocate® designation, expanding Craystar's mission to include travelers who need accessible planning — from mobility accommodations to sensory-friendly itineraries to medical-equipment logistics. Accessible travel is a category that's chronically underserved by the industry, and Craystar is committed to making it part of every conversation.

"Every family deserves a vacation planned by someone who actually knows the destination. That's what I'm here for — and you never pay a planning fee, ever."

Craystar is an independent advisor with Riverdale Travel, an established host agency that provides industry credentials, supplier relationships, and back-office support. That partnership is what lets a one-person agency like Craystar deliver perks and pricing typically reserved for major travel networks.

Reach Eric directly by call or text at 417-771-0910, or email eric.crayton@craystartravelagency.com. Hours are Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm CST.

How we work

What Craystar believes about your vacation.

01 / Free, always

Suppliers pay us, never you.

Our planning service is genuinely free. There are no hidden fees, no markup, and no upcharge. Cruise lines, resorts, and parks pay our commission — your pricing is the same as booking direct, and often better.

02 / Personal

Built for your party, not a template.

Every quote is custom. Group size, accessibility needs, dining preferences, dietary restrictions, anniversary surprises — we plan around the way your family actually travels.

03 / Connected

Perks the public can't access.

Onboard credits, room upgrades, dining packages, exclusive promotions — supplier-only perks come standard when available. That's the value of working with a credentialed advisor.

04 / Reachable

A real human, before, during, and after.

Booking is one phone call. So is help when a flight cancels, a port skips, or a Disney reservation needs adjusting. Eric answers — by call, text, or email.
