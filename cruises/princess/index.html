Common Questions

Frequently asked.

Does it cost more to book Princess through Craystar?

No. Pricing is identical to princess.com. Princess pays travel agencies a commission directly.

What is the Princess MedallionClass experience?

MedallionClass uses a small wearable device (the Medallion) to enable contactless boarding, automatic stateroom door unlock when you approach, food and drink delivery anywhere on the ship, location-based services for finding your party, and personalized recommendations through the Princess app.

Why is Princess known for Alaska?

Princess has the longest Alaska heritage of any major cruise line — operating Alaska sailings since 1969. Princess owns five wilderness lodges across Alaska (including Denali Princess Wilderness Lodge) and operates the Princess Rail service connecting their Whittier port to interior Alaska.

What does the Princess Plus / Premier package include?

Princess Plus adds beverage package (drinks under $15), Wi-Fi, crew gratuities, and other perks for a flat per-day add-on. Princess Premier upgrades to premium drinks (under $20), unlimited Wi-Fi devices, two specialty dining nights, premium photo package, and more.

How is Princess different from Royal Caribbean?

Princess is more destination-focused, more refined, and skews older (50-65 sweet spot). Royal Caribbean is more activity-focused with massive ships, waterparks, and high-energy entertainment.

I have an existing Princess booking. Can you take it over?

Possibly. Bookings can sometimes be transferred to a travel agent if booked within the last 60 days and not paid in full. There's no cost to transfer.
