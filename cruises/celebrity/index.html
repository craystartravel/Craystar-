Common Questions

Frequently asked.

Does it cost more to book Celebrity through a travel agent?

No. Pricing matches CelebrityCruises.com exactly. Celebrity pays travel agencies a commission directly.

What does Celebrity's Always Included fare cover?

Always Included is Celebrity's standard fare and bundles the Classic Drinks Package, Basic Wi-Fi, and crew gratuities into your cruise price. Upgrade options like Premium Drinks, Premium Wi-Fi, and shore excursion credits are available through Indulge or Elevate packages.

How is Celebrity different from Royal Caribbean?

Same parent company, very different brand. Celebrity targets a more refined, adults-leaning audience — smaller ships, elevated dining and service, fewer waterslides and family-noisy activities, and itineraries that emphasize destination immersion. Celebrity is premium-tier; Royal is contemporary.

Are Celebrity Edge-class ships worth the upgrade?

For most travelers, yes. Edge, Apex, Beyond, Ascent, and Xcel feature the Magic Carpet, Infinite Verandas, Eden, and four distinct dining rooms — genuinely different from anything else at sea.

How far in advance should we book?

For peak weeks, 9-12 months out is the sweet spot. For Alaska, book by January. For Galapagos, book 12+ months out — capacity is limited and sells out fast.

Can you book group cruises?

Yes. Group bookings (typically 8 staterooms minimum) often qualify for special amenities, onboard credit, group cocktail receptions, and reduced rates.
