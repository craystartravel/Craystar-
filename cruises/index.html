Accessible Cruising

SNG Certified Accessible Travel Advocate®

Craystar Travel Agency is proud to hold the SNG Certified Accessible Travel Advocate® credential through Special Needs Group / Special Needs at Sea — the leading global provider of mobility, oxygen, and medical equipment rentals for travelers, and a trusted partner of every major cruise line.

When you book your cruise with us, we can coordinate wheelchairs, scooters, power chairs, oxygen, hospital beds, patient lifts, and specialty equipment delivered directly to your stateroom before you board. We ask the right questions early — mobility, accessible stateroom options, dining access, sensory considerations, and shore-excursion needs — so every detail is handled before you step on the ship.

Traveling with a mobility, medical, or sensory need? Let's plan a cruise built around you.
