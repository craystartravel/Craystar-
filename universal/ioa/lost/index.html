Lost Continent · Islands of Adventure

The quiet land with the best lunch.

Lost Continent is IOA's pass-through area between Seuss Landing and Hogsmeade — and it's home to Mythos, consistently ranked as the best restaurant at Universal Orlando.

What's at Lost Continent

Mythos, the Mystic Fountain, and the atmosphere.

Lost Continent has shrunk over the years as Hogsmeade expanded, but it's still worth stopping for Mythos alone. Reservations are essential — they book out days in advance.

  • Mythos lunch reservations (book 1-2 weeks ahead)
  • Mystic Fountain interactive show
  • Dueling Dragons archway and entrance photo ops
  • Pause-and-breathe strategy between Hogsmeade and Jurassic
  • Best time of day to visit (lunch is the headline)
