The Express Pass Math

Premier hotels often pay for themselves.

Universal's Express Pass costs $90–$200+ per person, per day, depending on the season. Two adults staying three nights at a Premier hotel get Unlimited Express Pass included — and that benefit alone can be worth $1,000 or more.
