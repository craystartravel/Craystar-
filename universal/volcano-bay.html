Volcano Bay · Orlando, FL

Skip the line. Float the day.

Universal Orlando's water theme park — built around the 200-foot Krakatau volcano with the TapuTapu wearable that lets you queue without standing in line.

The Park

Built for the rest day you didn't know you needed.

Volcano Bay works as a one-day add to a Universal trip — or as your "lazy day" between marathon park days.

🌋

Krakatau Aqua Coaster

The signature ride — a water coaster through the inside of the volcano with launches and drops.

📿

TapuTapu Wearable

Reserve your slot in line with a tap, then go float somewhere else until your turn. Universal's best invention.

🌊

Waturi Beach

The wave pool at the foot of Krakatau — the heart of the park, with a sand beach and cabanas.

🏝️

Premium Cabanas

Reserved cabanas with food and drink service — worth the splurge in peak summer.

🏊

Slides for All Ages

From toddler-friendly Tot Tiki Reef to the freefall Ko'okiri Body Plunge — Volcano Bay covers every thrill level.

⚡

Express Pass at Volcano Bay

Universal Express Pass works here too — and on busy days, it's the fast track even with TapuTapu.
