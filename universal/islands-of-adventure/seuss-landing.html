What's at Seuss Landing

Family-friendly rides, charming details, and lunch at Green Eggs & Ham.

Seuss Landing is the best corner of IOA for parties with kids under 8 — the pace is slower, the heights are lower, and the theming is outstanding.

  • Rides for younger kids (minimal height requirements)
  • Green Eggs and Ham Cafe lunch timing
  • High in the Sky Trolley Train Ride strategy
  • Character meet-and-greets (Cat in the Hat, The Grinch)
  • Best timing for families (first thing in the morning)
