Universal Studios Hollywood · Los Angeles, CA

The real backlot. The original park.

Universal Hollywood is a working movie studio with a theme park built into it. The Studio Tour rolls past sets being filmed today — and the rest of the park spans Super Nintendo World, Hogsmeade, and Jurassic World.

What's at USH

Two parks of theming on a real working studio.

The Upper Lot and Lower Lot are connected by a series of escalators — and the Studio Tour is the experience you can't get anywhere else.

🎬

The Studio Tour

The signature attraction — a 60-minute tram ride through real sets, soundstages, and the Fast & Furious finale.
